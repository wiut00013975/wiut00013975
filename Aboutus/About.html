History of the company

2021 - Opening of the new press shop in the branch in Pitnak city, Khorezm region

2021 - Assembly of cars under the Chevrolet brand was launched in Gadjigabul, Azerbaijan

2020 - Asaka automobile plant "GM Uzbekistan" was awarded by General Motors with BIQ III certificate

2020 - Start of assembly of cars under the Chevrolet brand in accordance with the standards of General Motors in Kostanay, Kazakhstan.

2020 - A record number of cars was produced at the plants of "UzAuto Motors" JSC for the year - 280,000

2019 - Official presentation of the car Ravon Nexia R3 in Almaty, Kazakhstan

2019 - Opening of the service and trade center "UzAuto Motors Service & Trade-In Center", which makes it possible to exchange a used car for a new one with an additional payment under the Trade-In system

2019 - Motor rally Tashkent-Minsk

2019 - The enterprise was formed as a national brand and was renamed to «UzAuto Motors» JSC

2018 - "GM Uzbekistan" JSC launched “UzAuto Savdo” - the first online car purchase platform in Uzbekistan

2018 - Asaka automobile plant "GM Uzbekistan" was awarded by General Motors with BIQ III certificate

2018 - Production of 3 millionth car

2018 - Participation of the company at MIAS - an exhibition of automobiles and technologies

2018 - Start of production of Tracker model by SKD method

May 2018 - Has received the third degree of quality integration in the production processes of the plant - certificate BIQ III (Built-In Quality)

May 2017 - Start of assembly of the T250 in Kostanay, Kazakhstan

May 2017 - Launch of production of the Labo car with an extended cargo compartment

April 2017 - Launch of a new second generation Malibu car

December 2016 - Equipping the Cobalt export vehicles with the Era-Glonass emergency response system.

November 2016 - Equipping the export cars J200 and M300 with the emergency response system Era-Glonass.

August-September 2016g. - participation in the Moscow International Auto Show.

July 2016 - Equipping the T250 export vehicles with the Era-Glonass emergency response system

June 2016 - start of production of motor car Nexia (T250).

May 2016 - presentation of car Nexia (T250)

April 2016 - start of production of motor car Captiva (SORP).

October 2015 - presentation of the Ravon brand in Moscow.

December 2015 - the launch of production of motor car Lacetti with HBO.

March 2014 - start of production of the Chevrolet Orlando.

January 2014 - opening of the branch GM Uzbekistan in Pitnak.

November 2013 - start of production of motor car Lacetti II (Gentra).

September 2012 - start of serial production of motor car Chevrolet Cobalt.

March 2012 - start of large-block assembly of motor car Chevrolet Malibu.

September 2011 - start of a large-block assembly of the updated Chevrolet Captiva.

April 2011 - output of a 1,500,000-car.

August 2010 - start of production of motor car Spark.

November 2008 - output of a millionth car.

November 2008 - start of production of Lacetti.

June 2008 - start of production of the Nexia N150.

March 2008 - creation of joint venture "GM Uzbekistan".

December 2007 - start of production of Nexia and Matiz cars with EURO III engines.

October 2007 - start of a large-block assembly of cars Tacuma, Epica and Captiva.

July 2007 - output of a 750,000-car

May 2007 - an agreement on strategic cooperation between the Government of the Republic of Uzbekistan and GM Daewoo Auto & Technology was signed.

February 2006 - start of production of Damas B150 in place of the B100.

October 2005 - the issue of a 500,000-car.

December 2004 - Matiz Best started production with a 1.0 liter engine and automatic transmission.

June 2004 - the International Quality Summit - New York International Prize for Quality, Leadership, Advanced Technologies and Innovations.

April 2003 - certification of quality system for compliance with the international standard ISO: 9001: 2000.

August 2003 - start of large-block assembly of the Lacetti.

March 2003 - Matiz car was nominated by the "Motor Car of the Year" on the market of the Russian Federation.

August 2002 - start of production of Nexia car with a DOHC engine.

August 2001 - start of production of Matiz car.

May 2001 - the release of a 250,000-car.

December 1999 - certification of the quality system for compliance with the international standard ISO: 9001: 1998.

June 1996 - start of production of Tico and Nexia cars.

March 1996 - start of production of Damas.

June 1994 - commencement of construction of the plant in Asaka.

March 1993 - registration of JV "UzDaewooAuto".

June 1992 - President I. Karimov visit to Korea and to Daewoo Motor.
